Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we protect your data and respect your privacy with complete transparency.

  • Provide transparent privacy practices

What We DON'T Do

  • Store or upload your personal files
  • Track you across other websites

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about which tools are used most frequently, general geographic region (country level), browser type, and device type
  • Performance Data: Page load times and error reports to help us improve our services
  • Technical Information: IP address (not stored permanently), browser version, operating system, screen resolution

Information You Provide

We only collect personal information when you voluntarily provide it:

  • Contact Forms: Name, email address, and message content when you contact us
  • Feedback: Any feedback or suggestions you choose to share
  • Newsletter: Email address if you opt-in to receive updates (optional)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Content of files you process through our tools
  • Personal documents, code, or text you input into our tools
  • Detailed browsing history or cross-site tracking data
  • Biometric or sensitive personal information

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Service Provision

  • Provide and maintain our tools and website functionality
  • Ensure tools work properly across different browsers and devices
  • Save your theme preferences (dark/light mode) locally

Improvement & Analytics

  • Understand which tools are most valuable to users
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Plan new features based on usage patterns

Communication

  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Send occasional updates if you've opted in
  • Notify you of important service changes or security updates

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Limited Sharing Scenarios

We may share information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Security Protection: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of users or the public
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users would be notified)
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate our website (see Third-Party Services section)

Anonymous Data

We may share aggregated, anonymized usage statistics that cannot be used to identify individual users, such as:

  • "X% of users prefer the code formatter tool"
  • "Most traffic comes from mobile devices"
  • General geographic distribution of users

4. Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Hosting: Our website is hosted on secure, regularly updated servers
  • Access Controls: Limited access to any collected data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Updates: We keep all software and security measures up to date

Data Minimization

  • We collect only the minimum data necessary for functionality
  • Most user data never leaves your device
  • We regularly review and delete unnecessary data

Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a security breach affecting personal data, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Report to relevant authorities as required by law
  • Take immediate steps to secure the breach and prevent future incidents

5. Local Data Processing

Key Privacy Feature: All tool processing happens locally in your browser.

What This Means

  • Your files, code, text, and calculations never leave your device
  • We cannot see, store, or access the content you process
  • Your data remains private even if our servers are compromised
  • You maintain complete control over your information

Browser Storage

Some data is temporarily stored in your browser for functionality:

  • Theme Preferences: Dark/light mode setting
  • Calculator History: Recent calculations (cleared when you close the browser)
  • Form Data: Temporarily cached for user convenience

You can clear this data anytime through your browser settings.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

Essential Storage

We use minimal local storage for essential functionality:

  • Theme Preference: Remembers your dark/light mode choice
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage tracking (can be disabled)

No Tracking Cookies

We do not use:

  • Advertising cookies
  • Cross-site tracking cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Persistent user identification cookies

Your Control

You can control cookies and local storage through:

  • Browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Privacy/incognito mode for temporary sessions
  • Browser developer tools to inspect stored data

7. Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services, all carefully selected for privacy compliance:

Content Delivery (CDN)

  • Purpose: Faster loading of fonts and icons
  • Data: Basic connection information only
  • Providers: Google Fonts, jsDelivr, Font Awesome

Analytics (Optional)

  • Purpose: Understanding website usage patterns
  • Data: Anonymous, aggregated statistics only
  • Controls: Respects "Do Not Track" browser settings

Hosting & Security

  • Hosting Provider: Secure, privacy-compliant hosting
  • SSL Certificate: Encrypted connections
  • DDoS Protection: Security without data collection

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access & Portability

  • Right to Access: Request information about data we have about you
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format

Control & Correction

  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data

Communication Preferences

  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from any communications
  • Object to Processing: Object to certain uses of your data

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We will respond within 30 days and may request identity verification for security purposes.

9. International Users

Global Compliance

We respect privacy laws worldwide, including:

  • GDPR (EU): European General Data Protection Regulation
  • CCPA (California): California Consumer Privacy Act
  • PIPEDA (Canada): Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act
  • Other Regional Laws: We adapt to applicable local privacy requirements

Data Transfers

Since most processing happens locally in your browser, international data transfers are minimal. Any necessary transfers are protected by:

  • Appropriate safeguards and security measures
  • Compliance with relevant transfer mechanisms
  • Limiting transfers to essential services only

10. Policy Updates

Notification of Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect:

  • Changes in our practices or services
  • Legal or regulatory requirements
  • User feedback and improvements

How We Notify You

  • Website Banner: Prominent notice of significant changes
  • Email Notification: If you've opted in to receive updates
  • Updated Date: Always displayed at the top of this policy

Your Continued Use

Continued use of our website after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms. If you disagree with changes, please discontinue use and contact us with concerns.
